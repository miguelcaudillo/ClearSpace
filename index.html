FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What new customers usually ask before booking.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Nope. Many customers leave us entry instructions (a key, a code, or a smart lock) and come home to a clean house. Whatever you're comfortable with.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes — we arrive with everything we need. If you'd prefer specific products (unscented, eco-friendly, or your own), just let us know and we're happy to use them.

Are you licensed and insured?

We're a new local business setting up our license and business insurance; we'll update this page with the details as soon as they're active. Ask us for the current status anytime.

How does your pricing work?

Flat rate, not hourly. Use our calculator to see your exact price based on size, rooms, service type, and frequency — what you see is what you pay, no surprises.

What's the difference between a standard and a deep clean?

Standard keeps an already-maintained home fresh (kitchen, bathrooms, dusting, floors). Deep adds baseboards, vents, tile, behind furniture, and more — ideal for a first visit or a seasonal reset.

Do you offer service in Spanish?

Absolutely — we serve you in English and Spanish, from the quote to the crew. Switch the whole site with the EN/ES button up top.

What if I'm not satisfied?

Tell us within 48 hours and we'll come back and re-clean the area free. Our goal is for walking into your home to feel amazing, every time.
