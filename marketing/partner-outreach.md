# ClearSpace — B2B Partner Outreach Playbook

Your best, most reliable growth channel isn't one-off homeowners — it's **repeat
business partners**: realtors, property managers, and short-term-rental (Airbnb/VRBO)
hosts who need cleans over and over. One good partner can be worth 20 one-time
customers. Hand them `partners-sheet.html` (printed or the PDF) and use the scripts below.

**Who to target & where to find them**
- **Realtors** — local brokerage offices (Keller Williams, RE/MAX, Century 21 in the SL valley), or search "West Jordan realtor" and DM/email. Newer agents building their business are the most receptive.
- **Property managers** — property-management companies in Salt Lake County; also independent landlords on Facebook rental groups.
- **Airbnb / STR hosts** — the gold mine. Airbnb "co-host" / cleaning gigs, local host Facebook groups ("Salt Lake Airbnb Hosts"), or message listings directly. They need turnovers *forever*.

## The follow-up (this is where partnerships are won)
- If they say "maybe later," ask: **"Can I check back in two weeks?"** — then actually do.
- After a trial job, ask: **"Did that make your job easier? Want me on your regular rotation?"**
- Deliver 2–3 flawless jobs and **ask for a referral to one other agent/host** — this is how a partner network compounds.

## Why this beats chasing homeowners
- **Repeat, predictable revenue** — a host with 3 units can mean 6–12 cleans/month, forever.
- **Lower acquisition cost** — win one relationship, get dozens of jobs.
- **Warm referrals** — realtors and hosts know other realtors and hosts.
- **You already have the tools** — move-out checklists, photo-walkthrough process, and the partner sheet. This is your unfair advantage; use it.
