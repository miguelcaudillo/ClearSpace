# ClearSpace — Referral Program

Word of mouth is the #1 way cleaning businesses grow. A happy customer already
trusts you — a small, mutual incentive turns them into your best salesperson.

## The offer
**Give $20, Get $20.**
- Your customer refers a friend → the **friend gets $20 off** their first clean.
- The referring customer gets **$20 off** their next clean.
- **No limit** — they can refer as many friends as they like.

*(Change the amounts anytime in `pricing-config.js` → `business.referral`. Update the
printed card `marketing/print/referral-card.html` to match, since print can't read config.)*

## How to run it (simple, no software needed)
1. **Hand a referral card** (`marketing/print/referral-card.html`) to every customer at the end of a clean — tuck one in with your thank-you.
2. When a new lead says **"[Name] referred me,"** write it down. Apply $20 off their first clean.
3. **Credit the referrer** $20 off their next visit. A quick note in your dashboard/notes is enough to track it.
4. Text the referrer a thank-you (below) — it makes them refer again.

## Messaging

**Ask a happy customer to refer (the referral message is in your quote engine — `node tools/quote-engine.js` → "REFERRAL ASK"):**
> Hi [name]! So glad you loved your clean. 💚 Know a friend who could use one? Send them our way — they get $20 off their first clean, and you get $20 off your next one. Just have them mention your name when they book!

**Thank a referrer after their friend books (EN):**
> [Name], your friend [friend] just booked — thank you for the referral! 💚 Your $20 credit is on your next clean. You're the best.

**Thank a referrer (ES):**
> [Name], ¡su amigo/a [friend] acaba de reservar — gracias por recomendarnos! 💚 Su crédito de $20 va en su próxima limpieza. ¡Mil gracias!

## When to ask
- **Best moment:** right after a clean they loved, or when they leave a 5-star review (a happy customer is a referring customer).
- **Pair it with reviews:** the review request and referral ask work great back-to-back — "loved it? here's how to leave a review… and here's how to earn $20 by sending a friend."

## Why this compounds
Every new customer becomes a potential referrer, so a good referral program grows
on itself. Combined with your B2B partners and Google reviews, it's the third leg
of cheap, durable growth — no ad spend required.
