## Posting rhythm for launch month
- **Week 1:** Post 1 (launch) on FB + IG + Nextdoor. Pin it.
- **Week 2:** Post 2 (how it works).
- **Week 3:** Post 3 (move-out) — or a real before/after if you have one.
- **Week 4:** Post 5 (social proof) once you have a review or two.
- Then: 1–2 posts/week, mostly real before/afters. Consistency beats perfection.
