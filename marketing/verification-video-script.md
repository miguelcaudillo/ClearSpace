# ClearSpace — Google Verification Video Script

Google verifies new service-area businesses with a **single, continuous video** (no cuts, no edits) recorded inside the Google Maps / Business Profile app when it prompts you. Goal: prove three things — (1) you operate in your service area, (2) you have real cleaning equipment/supplies, (3) you manage the business. Aim for **60–90 seconds**.

## Before you hit record
- Film in **landscape** (phone sideways), good lighting.
- Stage your gear first so it's ready: cleaning caddy/supplies, vacuum, mop/bucket, and — if you have any — a branded shirt, magnet/sign, business cards, or printed invoices/checklists. (You can print the `docs/checklists.html` sheet to show as a branded document.)
- If you have a work vehicle, start outside near it. If not, start at your front door/driveway — that's fine for a home-based service business; Google does **not** publish your address.
- Do it in **one take**. If you fumble, just stop and start over — don't try to edit.

## Shot list + what to say (one continuous take)

**① Outside / starting point (0:00–0:15)**
- *Show:* the street or your driveway/vehicle, then walk toward your supplies.
- *Say:* "Hi, this is Miguel with ClearSpace Home Cleaning. We're a house cleaning company based here in West Jordan, Utah, serving the Salt Lake valley. Let me show you our operation."

**② The equipment (0:15–0:40)**
- *Show:* pan slowly across your actual supplies — vacuum, mop and bucket, cleaning caddy, sprays, cloths, gloves. Pick up a couple of items as you go.
- *Say:* "Here's the equipment we bring to every job — our vacuum, mop, and full cleaning caddy with all our supplies. Everything we need for standard, deep, and move-out cleans."

**③ The branding / paperwork (0:40–1:00)**
- *Show:* whatever branded or business material you have — a shirt/logo, business cards, a printed checklist or invoice, or your phone showing the website (miguelcaudillo.github.io/ClearSpace).
- *Say:* "This is our room-by-room checklist we complete on every job, and here's our website where customers get an instant price. Our phone number is (801) 433-7342."

**④ Close (1:00–1:15)**
- *Show:* you, holding a supply or standing by the vehicle/door.
- *Say:* "That's ClearSpace Home Cleaning, serving West Jordan, Salt Lake City, and the whole valley. Thanks for verifying us."

## Tips that get videos approved
- **Show, don't just tell** — Google's reviewers care most about *seeing* real equipment and materials. The supplies pan (②) is the most important part.
- Keep the camera **steady and slow**; let each item stay on screen for a beat.
- Say the **business name and city out loud** at least once — you've got it in ① and ④.
- If you own a work vehicle, showing it (even without a wrap/logo) is strong evidence.
- Don't worry about being polished. Honest and clear beats scripted-perfect.

## After you submit
- Approval usually takes **a few days** (sometimes faster). Google emails you.
- If it's **rejected**, you can retry — the common fix is showing more real equipment/branded materials, or filming closer to your actual operating base. Redo the supplies section more thoroughly.
- Once approved, everything you pre-filled goes live. Then: upload photos, publish Post 1, and grab your review link.
