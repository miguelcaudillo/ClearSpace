## 5. Setup order (do these in sequence)

1. Create the profile at business.google.com → verify (postcard/video — Google chooses).
2. Paste the description, set categories, hours, service area (the 8 cities — service-area business, hide street address).
3. Add the service list above.
4. Upload 10+ photos (logo, crew, before/afters, supplies — real photos beat stock).
5. Copy your review link into `pricing-config.js` (`reviewUrl`) and into the GHL review workflow.
6. Publish Post 1, then keep a 1–2×/week cadence with Posts 2–5 as templates.
