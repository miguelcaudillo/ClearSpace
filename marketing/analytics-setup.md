or where they come from — so you're guessing whether your flyers, posts, and Google
listing are working. Analytics fixes that. Here's the simplest privacy-friendly option
(free, no cookie-consent banner needed).

**Why GoatCounter:** free for personal/small use, no cookies, no consent banner required, and it shows exactly what you need — visits, top pages, and referrers (so you can tell if traffic came from Google, Facebook, or your flyers).

## Alternative: Cloudflare Web Analytics (also free, no cookies)
If you put your site on a domain through Cloudflare later, its Web Analytics is
excellent and equally privacy-friendly. Worth revisiting when you buy a domain.

## What to watch once it's on
- **Visitors/week** — is it growing as you do GBP, posts, and flyers?
- **Top pages** — are people reaching the calculator and quote form?
- **Referrers** — which channel actually sends people (Google? Nextdoor? direct from a flyer?). Double down on what works.
