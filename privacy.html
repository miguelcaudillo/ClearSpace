Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026 · Template — have an attorney review before relying on it.

ClearSpace Home Cleaning ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Information you give us: your name, phone, email, address, and home details when you request a quote or book.
  • Basic site-usage data (pages visited), if we enable analytics in the future.

How we use it

  • To prepare your quote, schedule, and provide cleaning service.
  • To contact you by phone, text, or email about your request.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Who we share it with

Only with service providers that help us operate (for example, the service that processes our quote form, and if applicable our messaging tool). They use the data only to provide that service to us.

Cookies & local storage

Our site uses your browser's local storage to remember your language preference and for internal features. We do not use advertising-tracking cookies.

Your choices

You can ask us to see, correct, or delete the information you gave us — just contact us and we'll take care of it. You can ask us to stop contacting you anytime.
